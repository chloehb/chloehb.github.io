mOOving up

Chloe Bentley, University of Michigan, Fall 2023

In a team of five students, including Alyvia Osbourne, Mathurin Gagnon, Megan Chow, and Surya Sultes, we developed this cow/space themed action-adventure game. In mOOving up, the player acts as a deployed UFO pilot sent to Earth by a mysterious entity known as Madame Bessy. Exploring a series of increasingly hazardous regions, the pilot is tasked with retrieving Madame Bessy's fashion items and collect as many cows as possible to bring back to their home planet.

Technology used: C#, Unity
