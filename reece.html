Reece - Superior Ghost Control

Chloe Bentley, University of Michigan, Fall 2023

Project Description

Over the course of less than two weeks, I came up with and developed this mini puzzle-adventure game, focused around the character's use of a flashlight. In the game, the user plays as Reece, a humane ghost hunter, who has been called in to a haunted mansion where the inhabiting ghosts keep locking doors and stealing the keys. Using her flashlight, she navigates through each room of the mansion, herds ghosts into an inescapable cage where they can no longer cause havoc, and retrieves the room keys. The background artwork was designed by my sibling, Sydney Bentley, and the remaining art/characters were designed by me.

Technology used: C#, Unity
